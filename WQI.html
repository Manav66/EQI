Water Quality Index

Know More
Water quality index (WQI) provides a single number that expresses the overall water quality, at a certain location and time, based on several water quality parameters. The WQI is basically a mathematical means of calculating a single value from multiple test results. A number of indices have been developed to summarize water quality data in an easily expressible and easily understood format.

Major indicators of water quality are -
  • Hydrogen ion concentration (pH)
  • Electrical conductivity (EC)
  • Alkalinity (AK)
  • Chloride (Cl−)
  • Nitrate (NO−3)
